Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website?

Workaround to get Widevine and PlayReady content keys

What is PSSH?

Protection Scheme Specific Header (PSSH)

Is it L1?

All provided Widevine devices are L3. There many be occasions where we add a L1, in such cases, this will be announced in the Discord server.

What does 'Error headers' means?

Your headers are not in yaml format or JSON Format. for Yaml formate Validate your headers here: http://yaml-online-parser.appspot.com

What does 'Wrong PSSH' means?

Your PSSH is wrong. validate your PSSH first on https://integration.widevine.com/diagnostics under "View Widevine PSSH"

What to do when there is no PSSH in the mpd?

Get kid from ism/mpd/media then make pssh from kid on https://integration.widevine.com/diagnostics under "Generate widevine PSSH". Also read: https://forum.videohelp.com/threads/401717-How-are-you-going-to-respond-to-widevine-l3-decryptor-s-death-at-May-31st/page15#post2639870

Can it support X site?

It should support any site that uses Widevine/PlayReady, however we do have a list of sites we do not allow our provided devices to be used with. If the site uses custom/non-standard requests with the license server, you will need to implement this yourself, ex: modifying the getwvkeys script.
